# Princeton Plasma Physics Laboratory (PPPL)

- Homepage: https://www.pppl.gov/

## About

> The U.S. [**Department of Energy**](https://www.energy.gov/)’s Princeton Plasma Physics Laboratory (PPPL) is mastering the art of using [**plasma**](https://youtu.be/M8cSQltH6TU?si=DYmMMtcfpa_VJE-P) — the fourth state of matter — to solve some of the world’s toughest science and technology challenges. That’s why public and private researchers worldwide look to PPPL for guidance on plasma science and related engineering challenges.
>
