# Guide to Cold Emailing

*(Adapted from the Columbia WiCs Organization’s “Guide to Cold Emailing” Page)*

## Finding a Research Mentor

- Review the APAM faculty list
    - [Applied Physics & Applied Mathematics Faculty Research](https://www.apam.columbia.edu/research-5)
- Learn about different labs and centers to find one that aligns with your research interests
    - See [](Labs%20&%20Centers%20260a2142cede80499f59d8cdff0122e6.md) to view some examples of the APAM labs at Columbia
