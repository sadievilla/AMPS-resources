# Columbia Electrochemical Energy Center

- Homepage: https://ceec.engineering.columbia.edu/

## About

> *The Columbia Electrochemical Energy Center (CEEC) is using a multiscale approach to discover groundbreaking technology and accelerate commercialization. CEEC joins together faculty and researchers from across the School of Engineering and Applied Sciences who study electrochemical energy with interests ranging from electrons to devices to systems.*
> 

## Research

- [Batteries for electric vehicles](https://ceec.engineering.columbia.edu/research-vision/batteries-EV)
- [Electrolyzers for Producing Carbon-Neutral Fuels and Chemicals](https://ceec.engineering.columbia.edu/content/electrolyzers-fuels-and-chemicals)
- [Grid-level storage](https://ceec.engineering.columbia.edu/research-vision/grid-storage)
- [Alleviating energy poverty](https://ceec.engineering.columbia.edu/research-vision/alleviating-energy-poverty)
- [Clean urban buildings](https://ceec.engineering.columbia.edu/research-vision/urban-building)

# **Center for Ubiquitous Connectivity (CUbiC)**

- Homepage: https://cubic.engineering.columbia.edu/content/about-cubic

## About

> *CUbiC is one of the seven research center under the [Joint University Microelectronics Program 2.0 (JUMP 2.0) initiative](https://www.src.org/program/jump2/), an SRC-led public-private partnership in cooperation with [DARPA](https://www.darpa.mil/), co-sponsored by the Semiconductor Research Corporation (SRC), DARPA, the commercial semiconductor industry, and the defense industrial base.*
> 

## Projects

- [Connectivity Networks and Systems](https://cubic.engineering.columbia.edu/content/theme-1-connectivity-networks-and-systems)
- [Wireline and Lightwave Interconnects](https://cubic.engineering.columbia.edu/content/theme-2-wireline-and-lightwave-interconnects)
- [Wireless Circuits and Technology](https://cubic.engineering.columbia.edu/content/theme-3-wireless-circuits-and-technology)
